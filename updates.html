v3.10.00

🏆 Latest New Content Balance

Feb 27, 2026  ·  World Baseball Classic Commemorative Update

🌍 World Baseball Classic Cards

  • A new set of exclusive Special Cards featuring players participating in the 2026 World Baseball Classic has been added.
  • WBC Cards can be added to any team regardless of team affiliation.
  • Cards receive performance-based bonus effects tied directly to real-life tournament results — stat boosts increase as the player performs well in actual WBC games.
  • WBC Cards have reduced growth requirements, making lineup upgrades more accessible.

🎫 World Baseball Classic Call-Up & Events

  • Complete Daily Missions to earn event rewards tied to the real-world WBC schedule.
  • Advance through milestone tiers via the Event Pass.
  • Use Event Scout Tickets to recruit from the WBC-themed scout pool.
  • Interactive events are added as actual WBC games are played, keeping in-game content synchronized with the real tournament.
  • New stadiums recreating WBC venues have been added for increased immersion.

🌟 World Baseball Classic Ambassador

  • A new WBC Ambassador feature has been added, highlighting global cover athletes including Jung Hoo Lee, Bryce Harper, and Yoshinobu Yamamoto.

⚙️ Improvements

  • (FA) Signature & Impact Cards: The first skill slot can now be locked to prevent unintended changes when using Skill Change Tickets.
  • Synergy Balance Revamp: Team synergy effects have been rebalanced for improved competitive fairness.
  • Senior Mission UI Improvements: The Senior Mission interface has been updated for clearer progression tracking.
  • Live Match (Amateur): The number of innings played in the Amateur Channel has been adjusted.

Signature Black Balance

Jan 28, 2026  ·  Signature Black Outfield Expansion & Balance Adjustments

⚫ Signature Black Card Expansion

  • Outfield positions (LF / CF / RF) have been added to the list of eligible Signature Black Card positions.

  • The Live Support Event is active during this period — check Main Screen > Inbox > Notification for current event details.
  • The Ranked Battle Support Box can be purchased once per account during Seasons 49, 50, and 51.

🐛 Bug Fixes

  • Potential items used on player cards affected by the Rarity Boost issue (where additional Potential unlocked unexpectedly) have been returned.
  • Potential Notes used excessively due to a display issue on leveling up Moment Card Potentials beyond S+ have been returned.

v3.08.00

New Content Signature Black

Dec 16, 2025  ·  New Signature Black Cards & New Special Cards

This update was split into two parts. Part 1 covers Signature Black Cards; Part 2 covers new Special Cards and content improvements.

⚫ Part 1 — New Signature Black Cards

  • Signature Black Cards are a new top-tier card type recruited by upgrading eligible Signature Cards that meet growth requirements.
  • FA Signature Cards can become FA Signature Black Cards under the same system.
  • Upon reaching Upgrade Lv. 10, a +2 boost is applied to all stats, for a maximum +2 OVR from Upgrades. (Position-specific boosts added in v3.09.00.)
  • Signature Black Upgrade requires 5 material cards to activate; can proceed with fewer if 100% success rate is achieved via items like Upgrade Success Guarantee Badge.
  • Upgrading from Lv. 0 to Lv. 1 requires a material card with OVR 120 or higher.
  • Special Training is available using Impact-grade or higher Special Cards. Each level boosts one chosen stat by +1 and raises Deck Score by +1.
  • Signature Black Special Training Retrain allows selecting a completed Special Training level to redo.
  • Training starts at Lv. 25 and can be developed up to Lv. 33. Training Boost starts at Lv. 10 and expands up to Lv. 18 using Advanced Training Boost Tickets and Points.
  • Signature Black Recruitment is available once per month; resets on the 1st. One discounted recruitment is also available, limited to once per account.
  • Transcendence system: Lv. 1–9 materials unchanged; Lv. 10+ material costs increase. Reaching Transcendence Lv. 9 allows expanding one base Potential to Lv. 15.

🃏 Part 2 — New Special Cards & Content Improvements

  • New 2025 Season end-of-year Special Cards added featuring standout performers.
  • Various content improvements and system quality-of-life changes implemented across card management and gameplay interfaces.

v3.05.00

New Feature Balance

Aug 26, 2025  ·  Rival Link, Called Game Rules & Senior Mission STEP 6

🔗 Rival Link

  • New [Rival Link] effect added — grants stat and Deck Score boosts to Season/Special Cards of the same team and same player as the selected Rival Pick.
  • When Rival Link is active, all stats increase by +2.
  • For Special Cards, an additional effect applies: +2 to maximum Deck Score based on card rarity (e.g., a Signature Card reaches Deck Score 28 with Rival Link).
  • Rival Link does not apply to FA Cards or Season/Special Cards from other teams.
  • Cards with Rival Link display a dedicated icon; check stat and Deck Score bonuses in the Player Card's Info tab.

🏟️ Called Game Rules (Live Match)

  • Live Matches can now end early when a team leads by 10+ runs in the 7th, 8th, or 9th inning (mercy rule).
  • Visiting team leading: Game ends if ahead by 10+ at the end of the bottom of the 7th, 8th, or 9th inning.
  • Home team leading: Game ends immediately if ahead by 10+ at any point during the bottom of the 7th, 8th, or 9th inning.
  • Opponent forfeits: Game ends if the leading team is ahead by 10+ runs at time of forfeit.
  • In-game notifications alert players when they are at risk of losing or in a position to win due to the score gap.

📋 Senior Mission STEP 6

  • Senior Mission STEP 6 has been added to help players understand gameplay mechanics and strengthen their team more quickly.

v3.04.00

New Mode 5-Tool Player

Jul 22, 2025  ·  Tiebreaker Ace Showdown & 5-Tool Player Moment Cards

⭐ 5-Tool Player Moment Cards

  • New [5-Tool Player] theme Moment Cards added, featuring elite players with standout hitting, defense, and baserunning tools.
  • Can be obtained as rewards from Tiebreaker Ace Showdown or through Moment Scout.

🆕 Swingman Moment Cards

  • New [Swingman] theme Moment Cards added alongside the 5-Tool Player set.

🏟️ Tiebreaker Ace Showdown Mode

  • A new competitive game mode introduced — Tiebreaker Ace Showdown.
  • Clubhouse Leader, Rival Pick, Deck Score, Synergy, and Position Training effects do not apply in this mode.
  • Boosted players have Upgrade Level fixed at 20; penalties to FLD or CTRL stats are reduced by half.
  • Penalty values vary by the difference between a player's primary and assigned position. Multi-position eligible players receive no positional penalties.
  • Two-way players can only be assigned as either Batter or Pitcher, not both simultaneously.

⚾ Extra Innings Rules Added

  • 10th inning: #1 batter leads off; #8 batter starts on 2nd and #9 batter on 1st.
  • 11th & 12th innings: The next batter in order leads off; the previous two batters are placed on 2nd and 1st respectively.

v3.03.00

New Content All-Star Event

Jun 24, 2025  ·  Hot Start Moment Cards & Ranked Slugger World Championship

🔥 Hot Start Moment Cards

  • New [Hot Start] Moment Cards added, featuring players who made a strong impact in the first half of the 2025 MLB season.
  • Cards unlock extra Potential levels at Transcendence Lv. 3, 6, and 9.
  • Moment Tier Skills can be obtained in the first skill slot at a certain rate when using Superior Skill Change Tickets.

🏆 Ranked Slugger World Championship

  • A special event celebrating the 2025 MLB All-Star Game — held in weekly rounds with up to 30 games per round.
  • Each player can only be used in one game per round (maximum 30 players total per week).
  • Rankings determined by the combined score of each player's top 3 game records in a given round.
  • Only Silver, Gold, and Platinum Balls appear — Normal Balls are excluded.
  • Players selected for the 2025 All-Star Game receive a Boost effect during the event.

🗓️ All-Star Lobby

  • Main Lobby for all teams updated to ATL's Truist Park to commemorate the 2025 MLB All-Star Game.

⚙️ Free Position Training Reset

  • A new Free Position Training Reset system has been added, allowing players to reallocate position training at no cost under specific conditions.

v3.02.00

Hall of Fame New Content

May 27, 2025  ·  Hall of Fame Cards & MLB Broadcast Commentary

🏛️ New Hall of Fame Cards

  • HOF Cards feature legendary players officially inducted into the MLB Hall of Fame — ranked above Signature Cards in rarity and prestige.
  • HOF Cards are not tied to a specific season; stats reflect full career performance.
  • Base team set to the team the player was inducted with. Players with multi-team legacies can use the Team Switch feature.
  • Up to 8 HOF Cards can be included in a lineup; one HOF Card from the Preferred Team is excluded from the limit.
  • Two-way HOF players count as one card even when in both pitcher and batter lineups.
  • Synergy effects scale with number of HOF Cards in the lineup.
  • HOF Cards are not eligible for FA Contracts or Rarity Boosts.
  • Legendary players available include Jackie Robinson, Derek Jeter, Greg Maddux, Don Sutton, Cal Ripken Jr., and Jim Thome.

🎙️ MLB Broadcast Commentary

  • Full TV broadcast commentary added with authentic MLB play-by-play. Caster Joe Davis and analyst Orel Hershiser provide commentary.

📋 Expert Mission STEP 5 & Other Improvements

  • Expert Mission STEP 5 added — complete STEP 4 to unlock. Earn HOF Coins, Ultimate Coins, and a Position Selective FA Signature Pack upon final completion.
  • Iron Tier skills no longer appear when using a Superior Skill Change Ticket.
  • Some Live Player Cards added to the default starter cards given to new teams.
